Caden Christel is an actor who has participated in many productions in Jacksonville, Florida. He has been involved with many Swoop Troupe shows, including [[production:2025 Ride the Cyclone]] (Ricky Potts), [[production:2025 Chicago]] (Billy Flynn), [[production:2026 Alice by Heart]] (Angus/Caterpillar), and [[production:2026 Sweeney Todd]] (Anthony Hope). He has also participated in other shows in the area, including [[production:2026 Into the Woods]] (Jack), [[production:2024 Be More Chill 2|Be More Chill]] (SQUIP), and [[production:2025 Cry-Baby: The Musical]] (Dupree, 2-day swing).
