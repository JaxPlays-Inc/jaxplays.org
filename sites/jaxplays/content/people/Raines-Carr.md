Raines Carr is a multidisciplinary theatre artist, director and educator whose work spans more than three decades in theatre, film and television. A Florida State University theatre graduate with an MFA in directing from the University of Alabama, Carr spent more than a decade in Los Angeles working in animation development and commercial casting before focusing his practice on theatre education and production.

Carr is the Managing Artistic Director of [[theatre:ABET - All Beaches Experimental Theatre]] in Atlantic Beach, where he leads actor-driven, community-engaged work rooted in adventurous storytelling. He also teaches theatre at Florida State College at Jacksonville and has directed award-winning college productions recognized by the Florida College System Activities Association.

As the co-creator and Founding Artistic Director of [[theatre:ShepardFest]], the Sam Shepard festival, Carr helps bring artists, students, scholars and audiences together around one of American theatre's defining voices.
