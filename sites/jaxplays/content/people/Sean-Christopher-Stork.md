Sean Christopher Stork is a baritone, composer, arranger, conductor and educator. His stage roles include Peter in *Hansel and Gretel*, Papageno in *The Magic Flute*, Procolo in *Viva la Mamma*, Marcello in *La Boheme* and Major General Stanley in *The Pirates of Penzance*.

Stork's concert and opera work includes appearances with the St. Augustine Community Orchestra and Chorus, Peach State Opera, Lakeland Opera, the Hudson Valley Orchestra and Chorale, and the Orlando Philharmonic Orchestra. He is also active in barbershop music, has premiered new works with First Coast Opera and Opera Orlando, and serves as artistic director of the Orange Blossom Chorus in Orlando.
