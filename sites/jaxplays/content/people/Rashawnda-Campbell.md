Rashawnda Campbell is a graduate of Douglas Anderson School of the Arts and earned a BFA in Musical Theatre from the University of West Florida. Her favorite roles include Felicia in *Memphis*, Nettie in *The Color Purple*, Rizzo in *Grease*, Asaka and Andrea in *Once on This Island*, Becky in [[production:2025 Waitress]] and Norma in [[production:2026 Victor Victoria|Victor/Victoria]].
