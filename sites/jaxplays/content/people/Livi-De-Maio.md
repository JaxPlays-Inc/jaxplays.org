Livi De Maio is a Jacksonville actor and musical theatre student at [[w:Penn State University]]. A Douglas Anderson School of the Arts graduate, she has been recognized with an Applause Award scholarship, the Florida Arts for Life Scholarship, and Top Honors at the Florida State Thespian Festival for Solo Musical. Her credits include Maria Rainer in [[production:2026 The Sound of Music|Limelight Theatre's *The Sound of Music*]] and Natasha in Douglas Anderson's *Natasha, Pierre & The Great Comet of 1812*, a performance recognized among the 2024 Applause Awards Outstanding Lead winners.
