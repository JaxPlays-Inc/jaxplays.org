Jonathan Leonard is a Jacksonville performer whose stage credits include *Waitress*, *Xanadu*, *Avenue Q*, *Evil Dead*, *Grease*, *I Love You, You're Perfect, Now Change*, *Hairspray*, *La Cage aux Folles*, *Man of La Mancha*, *Merrily We Roll Along* and *The Boy from Oz*. He also performs at St. John's Cathedral and has a background as a countertenor in classical music.

When not under stage lights, Jonathan is the proud owner and operator of the award-winning Vanity Furs Pet Salon and Hotel, serving the Jacksonville community for 17 years. So if he seems unusually comfortable with chaos, glitter, dramatic personalities and unconditional love… theatre and pet grooming are basically the same job.
