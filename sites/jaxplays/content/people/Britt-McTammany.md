Britt McTammany is a Florida-born film, television and visual media artist whose work spans cinematography, editing, animation, sound design, color, graphic design and photography. An Emmy Award-winning production and post-production professional, he has spent more than 35 years helping shape commercial, corporate, documentary and feature-film projects.

McTammany began his career as a music composer and became one of Florida's early Avid editors. As president of Digital Video Arts, he led a Jacksonville production company whose work served national brands and broadcast clients for more than two decades.

His screen work includes cinematography for *[The Artifice Girl](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt20859464/)*, the Franklin Ritch feature that premiered at Fantasia and went on to significant festival recognition. McTammany continues to work across film, design and image-making with a focus on cinematic, emotionally connected storytelling.
