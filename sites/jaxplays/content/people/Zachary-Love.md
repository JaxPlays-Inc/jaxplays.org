Zachary Love is a dancer with the Dance Works Company at Florida State College at Jacksonville. He has danced since middle school and continues to develop as a performer through movement, musical theatre and character-driven storytelling.

He is interested in voice acting, animation, music videos and film work that uses dance to tell stories, and he is continuing to explore new collaborative opportunities across the arts.
