Jeremiah Burch is a New York-based actor who is thrilled to be back at [[theatre:Alhambra Theatre & Dining|Alhambra]]. Previous credits include *Jersey Boys* as Bob Gaudio and *Cabaret* as Cliff. When not performing, he enjoys snowboarding and playing Fortnite Battle Royale. Jeremiah has a Bachelor of Fine Arts from [[w:LIU Post]].
