Chris Milligan is the Founding Executive/Artistic Director and Owner of [[theatre:Greenlight Theatre Company]]. Raised in community theatre in Slidell, Louisiana, he grew up learning the work onstage and backstage, building experience as a performer, lighting designer and stage manager. He earned a BA in Theatre Arts with a minor in Musical Theatre from Marymount Manhattan College and has performed in New York venues including 54 Below, Green Room 42, The West End, The Duplex, and The Actor's Temple, as well as regional theatres across the country and the national tour of The Wizard of Oz. His credits include Buddy in Elf, an Angel in Kinky Boots, and Mark in A Chorus Line. As an artist and educator, Chris aims to create memorable experiences that inspire students and audiences through class and performance.
