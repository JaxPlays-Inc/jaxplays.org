Daniel Torres is a Puerto Rico native who grew up in Jacksonville and graduated from Douglas Anderson School of the Arts in 1997.

His Broadway credits include *The Music Man* as Ewart Dunlop in the Barbershop Quartet, *Beautiful: The Carole King Musical*, where he played Barry Mann; *Evita*, where he served as the Che alternate for Ricky Martin; *The Pirate Queen*; *The Woman in White*; *Sweeney Todd*; *Tammy Faye*; and two seasons of *A Christmas Carol* at Madison Square Garden, including Scrooge at 18. Off-Broadway, Torres appeared as Juan in *Altar Boyz*. His national tour credits include *Wicked*.
