Teri "Tee" Anthony is a dancer and FSCJ danceWORKS company participant with training in ballet, hip hop, modern dance and improvisation. Her work includes danceWORKS pieces and student choreography concerts, and her artistic interests include choreography, photography, videography, creative writing, poetry, spoken word and music production.
