Jasmine Day Montgomery is a dancer and performer raised in Jacksonville after beginning ballet in San Jose, California. She studied ballet, pointe, modern, jazz, hip hop, break dancing, flamenco, African dance, tap and lyrical dance through arts schools, graduated from Douglas Anderson School of the Arts in 2005 and later trained in ballroom styles including waltz, tango, foxtrot, rumba, cha cha, swing, hustle, merengue, bachata and salsa. She teaches group classes and workshops at Studio 904.
