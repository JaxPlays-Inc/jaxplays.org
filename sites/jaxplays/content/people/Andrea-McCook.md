Andrea McCook has been enthralled with Sam Shepard since her introduction to his work decades ago as an undergraduate Theatre Arts major at the University of Santa Clara. As one of the founders of ShepardFest in 2021, she is thrilled that the festival is being resurrected under the leadership of Raines Carr and Mary Claire Branton. Andrea is a Professor of Theatre Arts at Flagler College, teaching acting and directing. She directs regularly on the Flagler main stage, where she directed *Trojan Women* in spring 2025 and is preparing for a production of [[production:2026 Vanya and Sonia and Masha and Spike|Vanya, Sonia, Masha and Spike]] in fall 2026. Most recently, she directed [[production:2026 God of Carnage|God of Carnage]] at Limelight Theatre in spring 2026, where she also directed *All My Sons* and [[production:2023 Storefront Church|Storefront Church]] in 2023 and appeared as Violet Weston in *August: Osage County* in 2022. She received her Master of Fine Arts in Graduate Acting from New York University Tisch School of the Arts and is a proud member of the National Alliance of Acting Teachers.
