Darvin Nelson is a Jacksonville performer, singer, songwriter and producer. He made his musical theatre debut in 2024 as one of The Drifters in *Beautiful: The Carole King Musical* and has since appeared in *Tarzan*, *Summer: The Donna Summer Musical* and *Funny Girl*.

Nelson began as a visual artist before focusing more deeply on music through the Ritz Voices and the FSCJ choir. He also works in the local film community and performs original music around Jacksonville.
