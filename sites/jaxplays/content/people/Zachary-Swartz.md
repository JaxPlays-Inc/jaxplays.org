Zachary Swartz is a rising freshman musical theatre major at Douglas Anderson School of the Arts and is proud to be part of Jacksonville's vibrant theatre community. He has been performing for many years and loves the opportunity to tell stories, connect with audiences and collaborate with fellow artists.

Some of Zachary's favorite stage experiences include performing at [[theatre:Alhambra Theatre & Dining]] in productions of *[[production:2024 Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat|Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat]]*, *[[production:2024 Gypsy|Gypsy]]* and *[[production:2024 A Christmas Story The Musical|A Christmas Story]]*. He has also enjoyed portraying Gomez Addams in *The Addams Family*, Conrad Birdie in *Bye Bye Birdie Jr.*, Buddy the Elf in *Elf Jr.* and Prince Oliver in *Between the Lines Jr.* at SET Studios, as well as Aladdin in *Aladdin* at Christ Church.

Zachary is grateful for the support of his family, teachers and mentors and looks forward to continuing to grow as a performer while pursuing his passion for musical theatre.
