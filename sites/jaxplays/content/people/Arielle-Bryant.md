Arielle Bryant is a Jacksonville-based multidisciplinary artist whose work began with dance and cheerleading at age three. She has trained for more than 20 years through studios and collegiate programs including Florida A&M University Essence Dance Theatre and FSCJ DanceWorks. Her performance and choreography credits include work with Mega Mind Productions, [[theatre:Players by the Sea]] and several Jacksonville directors.
