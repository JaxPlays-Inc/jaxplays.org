Cristina Bakhoum Sanchez is an American soprano whose work spans classic and contemporary opera. Born to Egyptian and Mexican parents, she brings a multilingual and culturally expansive point of view to her performances.

Her credits include Countess Almaviva in *Le nozze di Figaro*, the Foreign Princess in *Rusalka*, Mimi in *La Boheme*, Clementina in *The Desert Song*, Roya in Niloufar Nourbakhsh's *We the Innumerable*, and Kay in Scott Eyerly's *My Life Online*. She holds a graduate diploma from New England Conservatory, a Master of Music from Florida State University and a Bachelor of Music from Loyola University New Orleans.
