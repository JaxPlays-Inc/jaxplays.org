Josh Andrews is an ardent proponent of professional arts in this region and a cofounder of Lumen Repertory Theatre in Jacksonville, where the company has employed more than 110 professional artists since 2022. Josh extends his heartfelt gratitude to the many artists, collaborators, patrons and donors who continue to champion live professional theatre in Northeast Florida. He also thanks his close friends, his family, his wife Lydia and son Britton for their unwavering love and support.
