Rodney Holmes is an actor, director, educator and multidisciplinary theatre artist whose work spans performance, choreography, design and arts education. A longtime advocate for theatre education, he has helped launch educational programs for theatres across the country and is dedicated to creating spaces where young artists can build pride, professionalism, versatility and a lasting love of the arts. He has earned regional and national recognition for direction and choreography, and has worked with theatres in Colorado and Florida. Rodney is the artistic director and co-owner of [[theatre:Greenlight Theatre Company]], where he continues to champion communities where artists can grow and the arts can thrive.
