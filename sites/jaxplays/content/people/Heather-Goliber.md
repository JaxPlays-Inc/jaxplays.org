Heather Goliber is a theatre designer and stage professional based in the Jacksonville and Ponte Vedra Beach area. She trained at Rochester Institute of Technology and the National Technical Institute for the Deaf, developing design and construction skills through work with director Bruce Alverson and Philip Graneto. Her theatre work includes props design, stage management and assistant stage management.
