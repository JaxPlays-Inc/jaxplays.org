Jennifer O'Brien is a Northeast Florida performer with an A.S. degree in musical theatre from Florida School of the Arts. Her previous credits include *Honky Tonk Angels* with Blue Fire Theatre, *Bat Boy the Musical* and *Xanadu* at ABET, *Silence! The Musical*, *The Laramie Project* and *Company* at The 5 & Dime, and *Avenue Q* at Amelia Community Theatre.
