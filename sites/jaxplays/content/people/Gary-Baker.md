Gary Baker is a Northeast Florida actor whose local credits include Calliope in *Xanadu* at ABET, Nicky in *Avenue Q* at [[theatre:Amelia Community Theatre]] and Buffalo Bill in *Silence! The Musical* at [[theatre:The 5 & Dime]]. He brings a love of character work, comedy and community theatre to stages across the region.
