Kennedy Swartz is an 11-year-old singer, actor, and dancer from Jacksonville, Florida. She is thrilled to be part of the Jacksonville theatre community and loves every opportunity to bring characters to life on stage.

Kennedy's most recent credit includes Annie Who, as well as understudy to Cindy Lou Who, in the national tour of *How the Grinch Stole Christmas! The Musical*. She has loved her time on the [[theatre:Alhambra Theatre and Dining]] stage in *[[production:2024 Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat]]*, *[[production:2024 Gypsy]]*, and *[[production:2024 A Christmas Story The Musical]]*. Other memorable roles include Young Moana in *Moana, Jr.*, Jane in *Mary Poppins, Jr.*, JoJo in *Seussical Jr.*, and Ondine in *Between the Lines, Jr.* at SET Studios. She also made her New York City cabaret debut in BAA's *Broadway's Next Generation* at Don't Tell Mama.

When she's not on the stage, she enjoys spending time with her family, her cat Mochi, and friends. She would like to thank everyone for their love, encouragement, and support.
