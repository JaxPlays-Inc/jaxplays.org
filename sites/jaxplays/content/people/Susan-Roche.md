Susan Roche is a Northeast Florida theatre artist with more than 20 years of community theatre experience and over 100 productions as an actor, assistant director, stage manager, backstage technician, audio technician, videographer, photographer, house manager and board member. Favorite acting roles include Sister Mary Lazarus in *Sister Act*, Joann in *Company* and Hannah in *The Spitfire Grill*. Favorite stage management credits include *Bright Star*, *Xanadu*, *Disenchanted* and *Play On*.
