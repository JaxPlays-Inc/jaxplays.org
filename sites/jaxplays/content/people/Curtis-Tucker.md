Curtis Tucker joined First Coast Opera as artistic director in 2014 and now serves as general and artistic director. He previously led Opera Saratoga, Sorg Opera, and Whitewater Opera, and has conducted productions and concerts for companies and ensembles including Opera Memphis, Cincinnati Opera, Opera Saratoga, Syracuse Opera, Mobile Opera, Wichita Grand Opera, University of North Florida Opera Theater, and the Orlando Philharmonic Orchestra.

Tucker is also a composer whose one-act operas include *A Stranger's Tale*, *The Trial of B. B. Wolf*, and *Penelope Ann's Revenge*. He has taught at the University of Northern Colorado, Miami University - Middletown, and the Florida School of the Arts.
