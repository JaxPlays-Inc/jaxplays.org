WJCT Public Media is giving Jacksonville audiences a last-chance reminder for National Theatre Live's *[The Fifth Step](https://host.nxt.blackbaud.com/registration-form/?formId=779f0cee-95ef-4fa9-aae9-d35aebb1623e&envId=p-XXlyxX5pc0aMiATZRifMoQ&zone=usa)*, screening Wednesday, July 15, at WJCT Studios.<!--more-->

Written by David Ireland, *The Fifth Step* follows James, a longtime participant in Alcoholics Anonymous, as he becomes the sponsor of newcomer Luka. As the two men bond over coffee, recovery and uneasy confession, their fragile trust is tested when Luka approaches the fifth step, where buried truths begin to surface.

The acclaimed stage production stars Olivier Award winner Jack Lowden (*Slow Horses*, *Dunkirk*) and Emmy and BAFTA winner Martin Freeman (*The Hobbit*, *Sherlock*). Filmed live on stage, *The Fifth Step* brings the intimacy of live theatre to the big screen, balancing humor with emotionally charged drama.

Critics praised the West End run, with the Daily Mail calling it "[raw, excruciating and often alarmingly funny](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-14730277/PATRICK-MARMION-reviews-Fifth-Step-Soho-Place-Jack-Lowden-Martin-Freeman.html)" and the Evening Standard hailing it as "[two actors at the top of their game](https://www.standard.co.uk/culture/theatre/the-fifth-step-sohoplace-jack-lowden-martin-freeman-b1228567.html)."

Doors open at 6:30 p.m., and the screening begins at 7 p.m. Tickets are $15 for non-members and $10 for WJCT members who donate at least $60 per year. Popcorn and refreshments will be available for purchase.

**Event details**
- **When:** Wednesday, July 15, 2026, at 7 p.m.; doors open at 6:30 p.m.
- **Where:** WJCT Studios, 100 Festival Park Ave., Jacksonville, FL 32208
- **Tickets:** $15 for non-members; $10 for qualifying WJCT members

For tickets, visit [WJCT's registration page](https://host.nxt.blackbaud.com/registration-form/?formId=779f0cee-95ef-4fa9-aae9-d35aebb1623e&envId=p-XXlyxX5pc0aMiATZRifMoQ&zone=usa).
