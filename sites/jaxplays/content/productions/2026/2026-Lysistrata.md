In Aristophanes' *Lysistrata*, the women of Greece seize political power through a scandalous strike, forcing warring men to confront the cost of endless conflict.

All the Devils Shakespeare presented the comedy outdoors at Treaty Park, with free/pay-what-you-can performances directed by Dani Finch and Zaira Finderson.
