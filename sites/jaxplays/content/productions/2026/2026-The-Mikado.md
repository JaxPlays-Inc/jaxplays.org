*The Mikado* brings Gilbert and Sullivan’s topsy-turvy operetta to Lewis Auditorium with a fully staged production featuring orchestra and chorus.

Set in the fictional town of Titipu, the classic comic opera skewers power, vanity, and social absurdity with sparkling wit and famously hummable melodies.
