Feeling overlooked in their marriages and constrained by post-war London society, two housewives pool their savings for a women-only escape to Italy. To share the cost, they recruit two upper-class strangers, and the four women gradually move from friction to friendship among the wisteria and sunshine.

**Content advisory:** Adult content, alcohol/tobacco use.
