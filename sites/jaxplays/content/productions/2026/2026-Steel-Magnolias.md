River City Players presents *Steel Magnolias* as a Readers Theater performance, a dramatic reading in which actors read directly from scripts and use their voices to bring the story to life. Rather than relying on full staging, costumes, or props, the format centers expressive oral reading and narration.

Set in a small-town Louisiana beauty salon, *Steel Magnolias* follows six women whose friendships carry them through weddings, family change, illness, grief, and hard-earned laughter. In this reading format, the focus turns to Robert Harling's language, the rhythms of the ensemble, and the emotional resilience at the heart of the play.
