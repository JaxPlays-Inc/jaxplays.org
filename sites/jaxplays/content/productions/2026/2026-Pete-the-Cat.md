For Pete the Cat, life is an adventure no matter where you wind up. When the groovy blue cat meets the Biddle family, he gets everyone rocking except Jimmy, a highly organized second grader who needs a spark of inspiration.

Together, Jimmy and Pete set out on a friendship-filled adventure that takes them all the way to Paris and back in a VW Bus.
