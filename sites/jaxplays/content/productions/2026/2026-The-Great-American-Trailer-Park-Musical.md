There's a new tenant at Armadillo Acres, and her arrival sends Florida's most exclusive trailer park into a comic spiral.

*The Great American Trailer Park Musical* follows Pippi, a stripper on the run, as she comes between Jeannie, an agoraphobic woman who loves Dr. Phil, and Jeannie's tollbooth collector husband. Catalyst Theatre's inaugural production brings the musical's high-energy comedy and outsized Florida characters to [[venue:The Waterworks]] in St. Augustine.
