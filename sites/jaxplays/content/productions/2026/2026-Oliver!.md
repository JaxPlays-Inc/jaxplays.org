*Oliver!* follows orphan Oliver Twist from the workhouse into Victorian London’s criminal underworld, where he encounters the Artful Dodger, Fagin, Nancy, and Bill Sikes.

As he searches for safety and belonging, the musical balances hardship, humor, and heart while tracing a child’s struggle to survive.
