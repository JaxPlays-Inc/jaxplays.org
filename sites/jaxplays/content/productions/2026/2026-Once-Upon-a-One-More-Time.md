The fairy tales are getting a remix at [[theatre:Arts Collective Theatre]].

*Once Upon a One More Time* gathers Cinderella, Snow White and other storybook royals for a princess book club that gets upended when a fairy godmother drops *The Feminine Mystique* into their happily-ever-after world. Built around the songs of Britney Spears, the musical turns familiar fairy tale characters into a high-energy pop celebration of choice, independence and rewriting the ending.
