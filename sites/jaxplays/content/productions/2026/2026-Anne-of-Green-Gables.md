*Anne of Green Gables* follows Anne Shirley, a talkative and imaginative orphan mistakenly sent to live with Marilla and Matthew Cuthbert.

As Anne grows into her new home, her fierce heart and gift for wonder reshape Green Gables, her friendships, and the guarded adults who first expected someone else.
