Ren McCormack moves to a sleepy town where dancing is banned. Struggling to fit in, Ren challenges the status quo, sparking a dance rebellion among the youth and teaching the town to let loose. With classic '80s hits propelling the action, *Footloose* cuts loose in a story about shaking up the norm and finding your own rhythm.
