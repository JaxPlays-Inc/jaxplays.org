The Senior Theater Project and Amelia Musical Playhouse present *Summer Sensation 2026*, a two-performance summer program built around three short comedies, signature musical performances and comedy interludes.

The program includes *St Rita*, featuring Officer Wanda Spindle interviewing Ed Pavoni, a reluctantly retired detective hoping to return to the department, with his daughter Jerry supporting the effort. Another featured comedy, *Wailing Dave*, follows women trying to describe a flasher to a confused police officer.

Performances are Saturday, June 20 and Sunday, June 21, 2026, at 2:30 p.m. Tickets are $10, with reservations by phone at 904-277-3455. The email notes that there are no online ticket sales for this event.
