*Dear Evan Hansen* follows Evan, a high school senior whose social anxiety and a painful misunderstanding draw him into the aftermath of a classmate's death. As others believe Evan was secretly Connor Murphy's best friend, Evan becomes the center of a public story about connection and belonging.

Players by the Sea lists Friday and Saturday curtains at 8 p.m. and Sunday curtains at 3 p.m.

**Content advisory:** PBTS notes sensitive subject matter including suicide, drug use, bullying, and strong adult language. Recommended ages 14 and up.
