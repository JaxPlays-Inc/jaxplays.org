Ten strangers, each carrying a hidden past, arrive on a remote island at the invitation of an unseen host. One by one, they are killed according to a chilling nursery rhyme.

As the survivors realize the murderer must be among them, Agatha Christie's tense stage thriller becomes a desperate game of suspicion, fear, and survival.

**Content advisory:** Violence, suicide, and alcohol/tobacco use.
