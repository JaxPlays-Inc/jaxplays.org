*Hallmark(ish): The Unofficial Parody Musical* celebrates the holiday-movie formula with an affectionate comic twist. Holly, an overworked big-city executive, returns to her quirky small hometown to save Christmas and quickly finds herself caught between an old flame, a corporate merger, a cookie contest conspiracy and a moose on the loose.

The musical features original pop songs, holiday tropes and a sendup of the cozy Christmas movies audiences already know and love.
