Novelist Charles Condomine invites eccentric medium Madame Arcati to conduct a seance as research
for his next book. The experiment goes awry when the ghost of his late first wife, Elvira, is
accidentally summoned. Mischievous and jealous, Elvira is determined to reclaim Charles and wreak
havoc on his marriage to his current wife, Ruth. As tensions and ghostly pranks escalate, Charles
becomes hilariously entangled between two feuding wives, one living and one dead.
