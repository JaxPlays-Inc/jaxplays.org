Clever, charming and just a little too confident, Emma Woodhouse has sworn off marriage, but that won't stop her from arranging everyone else's. In this witty and romantic adaptation of Jane Austen's classic, Emma's well-meaning matchmaking quickly spirals into comic chaos, complete with surprise arrivals, tangled affections and lessons in love she never saw coming.
