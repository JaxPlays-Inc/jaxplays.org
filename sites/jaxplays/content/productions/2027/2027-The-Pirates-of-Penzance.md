Frederic is ready to leave his accidental pirate apprenticeship behind, but a leap-year technicality
threatens to keep him bound to the Pirate King. Add Major-General Stanley and a chorus of daughters,
and Gilbert and Sullivan's seaside comedy sails into glorious absurdity.
