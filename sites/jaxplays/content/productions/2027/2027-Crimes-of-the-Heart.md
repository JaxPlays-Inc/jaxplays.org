In Hazlehurst, Mississippi, the three Magrath sisters gather as their grandfather nears death in the hospital.

Lenny faces a lonely future, Meg returns after a failed singing career and Babe is out on bail after shooting her husband. Beth Henley's Pulitzer Prize-winning play finds humor and heart in a family pushed to its breaking point.
