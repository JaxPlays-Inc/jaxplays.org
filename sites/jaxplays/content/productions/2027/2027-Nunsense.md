*Nunsense* follows the Little Sisters of Hoboken as they stage a fundraiser to bury sisters accidentally poisoned by the convent cook, Sister Julia, Child of God.

Dan Goggin's long-running musical revue blends songs, jokes and convent chaos into a cheerful, irreverent comedy.
