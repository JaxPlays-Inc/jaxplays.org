Noah Bennett is an actor, playwright, educator, artist, and model based in Jacksonville, Florida. A graduate of the University of Florida and The Ohio State University, Bennett works across stage, screen, writing, and teaching.

His solo show *The Phil Mitchell Radio Hour* has played Ohio, Georgia, New York and North Carolina, earning critical acclaim and sold-out performances along the way. Bennett also teaches Theatre in the Humanities as an adjunct instructor at Florida State College at Jacksonville, following three years teaching at The Ohio State University.
