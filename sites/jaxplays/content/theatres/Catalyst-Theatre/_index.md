Catalyst Theatre is a professional producing theatre company based in St. Augustine, Florida.

Founded by Producing Artistic Director [[person:Sherrie Provence]], Catalyst Theatre describes its work as transformative storytelling designed to inspire, challenge and elevate audiences through bold artistic experiences. The company is rooted in hopeful and redemptive storytelling, with a focus on professional theatre that is artistically compelling and deeply human.

Catalyst begins as a nomadic theatre, with its first production at [[venue:The Waterworks]] in St. Augustine. The company's long-term vision includes a permanent home for theatre, film, television and live production, along with partnerships across Northeast Florida.
