[[theatre:A Classic Theatre]] will hold auditions for all roles in [[production:2026 Grand Horizons]], Bess Wohl's sharp comedy directed by [[person:David Buchman]].

Auditions will be held at 6:30 p.m. Monday, July 13, and 6:30 p.m. Tuesday, July 14, at [[venue:The Waterworks]], 184 San Marco Ave., in St. Augustine.

Performances will run Sept. 11, 12, 13, 18, 19 and 20.

After 50 years of marriage, Bill and Nancy calmly announce their divorce at the Grand Horizons retirement community, stunning their adult sons Brian and Ben. As the family descends into chaos, Ben's pregnant wife Jess and Brian's hookup Tommy are swept into the fray. With secrets unearthed and emotions unleashed, this comedy turns a quiet retirement into a full-blown family reckoning.

Auditions will consist of readings from the play. Performers should bring headshots and resumes, if available.

Actors who cannot attend but would like to audition may contact [calexander@aclassictheatre.org](mailto:calexander@aclassictheatre.org) or [davidbuchman@hotmail.com](mailto:davidbuchman@hotmail.com).

More information is available at [aclassictheatre.org](http://www.aclassictheatre.org).

### Roles

**Nancy French**

Female, 70s. A caring and resourceful former librarian. Tired of living only half a life, she asks Bill, her husband of 50 years, for a divorce to seek a new life and independence.

**Bill French**

Male, 70s. A retired pharmacist who seems unfazed at the idea of divorcing Nancy. Chock full of crude dad jokes, he is ready to start a new chapter of his life with his girlfriend, Carla.

**Ben French**

Male, late 30s-40s. Bill and Nancy's older son. A high-strung lawyer who is about to be a father for the first time. His family's dysfunction stirs up drama in his own marriage to Jess. The stress makes his emotions, and his eczema, flare up.

**Brian French**

Male, late 30s-40s. Bill and Nancy's younger son. A drama teacher who wears his heart on his sleeve. Although he is deeply affected by his parents' breakup, he just wants everyone to be happy. Actor must be comfortable with verbal and physical affection from another male actor.

**Jess French**

Female, late 20s-40s. Ben's very pregnant wife. An empathetic, yet outspoken, family therapist who tries to navigate the family's conflict. Strong-willed and outspoken, she is not afraid to stand up to her husband and brother-in-law.

**Tommy**

Male, 20s-mid 30s. A confident, blunt and flirtatious character who often adds a humorous, boundary-setting perspective to the situation. Actor must be comfortable with verbal and physical affection from another male actor.

**Carla**

Female, mid 50s-70s. Bill's girlfriend. A free spirit who is cheerful and warm, with a great sense of humor and comfortable offering her very candid perspectives.
