[[theatre:Orange Park Community Theatre]] will hold auditions for [[production:2026 Calendar Girls|Calendar Girls]] at 2 p.m. Saturday, June 27, and 6 p.m. Sunday, June 28.

Auditions will take place at [[venue:Orange Park Community Theatre]], 2900 Moody Ave., in Orange Park.

The company is auditioning for all roles. Auditions will consist of cold reads from the script, with sides provided, along with brief exercises in accents and group singing.

Audition sides are available through [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1qRqSQjntoxryySLDz2frjr5duwC-g0Ez/view?usp=sharing).

Performers can learn more and register through the [Cast98 audition page](https://app.cast98.com/shows/calendar-girls-2026-opct).

Questions may be directed to Matthew Fuhs at [mfuhs@me.com](mailto:mfuhs@me.com).
