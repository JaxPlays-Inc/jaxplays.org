Join [[theatre:The Island Theater]] for a delightfully dark and hilariously spooky production of *The Addams Family*!

Auditions will be held *The Addams Family Musical* at 7 p.m. Sunday, June 28, and Monday, June 29, 2026.

Performers should prepare 16-32 bars of a song from the show or in the style of the show that best highlights their voice and personality. Auditioners should bring recorded accompaniment.

Prepare 16–32 bars of a song from the show or in the style of the show that best highlights your voice and personality. Please come prepared with recorded accompaniment.

All auditioners should plan to participate in a dance audition. Come ready to move, have fun and embrace your inner Addams!

### Video Auditions

Unable to attend in person? Video auditions will be accepted through Friday, July 3rd.

The production runs Oct. 16-Nov. 1.

More information, including rehearsal dates and character descriptions, is available at [theislandtheater.com/addamsfamily](https://www.theislandtheater.com/addamsfamily).
