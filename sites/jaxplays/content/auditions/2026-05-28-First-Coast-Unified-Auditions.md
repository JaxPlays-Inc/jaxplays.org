Performers from across Northeast Florida will have a chance to audition for multiple theatre companies in one place when the First Coast Unified Auditions return Saturday, June 20, 2026, at [[theatre:Limelight Theatre]] in St. Augustine.<!--more-->

The annual audition event is open to actors of all experience levels and brings together directors and theatre representatives who are scouting talent for upcoming seasons throughout the region.

Auditions run from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. at Limelight Theatre, 11 Old Mission Ave., with pre-registered auditions scheduled first and walk-in auditions held later in the day. Minors who pre-register will audition at 10 a.m., followed by pre-registered adults at 11 a.m. After a noon lunch break, walk-in and general auditions will take place from 1 to 2 p.m.

Actors should prepare one of three audition options: a one-minute monologue, a 32-bar song cut, or both a one-minute monologue and a 32-bar song cut. Accompaniment will be provided for singers.

Performers who are not pre-registered should bring at least 10 copies of their headshot and resume. Walk-in auditions are first come, first served and are not guaranteed once all available slots are filled.

Pre-registration is available online through Limelight Theatre:

- [Minor pre-registration](https://limelight.ludus.com/volunteer/opportunity/10417)
- [Adult pre-registration](https://limelight.ludus.com/volunteer/opportunity/10418)

For questions, call the Limelight Theatre Box Office at (904) 825-1164.
