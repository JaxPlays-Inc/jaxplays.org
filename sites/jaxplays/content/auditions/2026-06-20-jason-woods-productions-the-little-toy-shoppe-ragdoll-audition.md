[[theatre:Jason Woods Productions]] is seeking an actress to play a ragdoll in [[production: 2026 The Little Toy Shoppe]] during the [Dickens On Centre](https://www.ameliaisland.com/dickens-on-centre/) festival in Amelia Island.

The role is paid. Rehearsal dates are to be announced, and performances are scheduled for Dec. 11-13, 2026.

For consideration, performers should submit a headshot, resume and reel or monologue to [nataliedrake6@gmail.com](mailto:nataliedrake6@gmail.com).

Use the subject line **RAGDOLL**.
