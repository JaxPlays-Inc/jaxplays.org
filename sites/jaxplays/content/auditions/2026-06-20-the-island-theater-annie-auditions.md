[[theatre:The Island Theater]] will hold auditions for *Annie* at 7 p.m. Sunday, Aug. 9, and Monday, Aug. 10.

The production runs Dec. 11-20. The production team includes director Caleb Wheeler, stage manager Holly Husovic, music director Caleb Wheeler and choreographer Sarah Mosier.

The Island Theater is pleased to revive *Annie*, its very first MainStage production.

For the vocal audition, performers should prepare a memorized and well-prepared 16- to 32-bar cut from a song in the style of the show. Those auditioning for Annie may choose to sing from the show or in the style of the show, and girls specifically auditioning for Annie should be prepared to sing "Tomorrow" or "Maybe." Auditioners should provide their own backing track.

Auditioners will also have the chance to do a cold read from the script. Please note that auditioners may not get to read for a character of choice; the team is looking for what performers can do with any character they are given.

Please be prepared to learn a short dance combination. Wear comfortable shoes or dance shoes. No flip-flops, Crocs, sandals or bare feet.
