The 5 & Dime Theatre Co. will hold auditions at 6 p.m. Sunday, June 21, for the roles of Carrie and Margaret White in [[production:2026 Carrie The Musical|Carrie: The Musical]].

The musical features music by Michael Gore, lyrics by Dean Pitchford and a book by Lawrence D. Cohen, based on the novel by Stephen King. The 5 & Dime production is directed by [[person:Caryl Butterley]], with music direction by [[person:Erin Barnes]] and choreography by [[person:Terry Star]].

This audition is only for the roles of Carrie and Margaret White. Auditions for all other roles in the show will be held in July, with the date to be announced soon.

More information about available roles, preparation and the rehearsal schedule is available at [the5anddime.org/audition](https://the5anddime.org/audition).

Members of The 5 & Dime's artistic team said they have had "deep and thoughtful conversations" about auditions and support open casting for any roles that are not predetermined by the playwright. The company encourages theatre artists to participate in auditions and looks forward to seeing the talent seeking roles in any 5 & Dime production.
