[[theatre:Theatre Jacksonville]] has announced audition dates for two upcoming productions.

Auditions for [[production:2026 The Lion in Winter|The Lion in Winter]] are scheduled for 6:30 p.m. Monday, June 8, 2026.

Auditions for [[production:2026 Come From Away|Come From Away]] are scheduled for 10 a.m. Saturday, August 8.

Details are available at [theatrejax.com/auditions](https://www.theatrejax.com/auditions).
