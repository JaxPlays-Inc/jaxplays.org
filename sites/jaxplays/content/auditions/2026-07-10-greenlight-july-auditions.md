[[theatre:Greenlight Theatre Company]] has announced July auditions for three upcoming musicals: *The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee*, *Avenue Q* and *The Rocky Horror Show Live!*.

Auditions for *The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee* will be held at 6 p.m. Monday, July 13. The production is presented in partnership with UNF's [[theatre:Swoop Troupe]].

Auditions for *Avenue Q* will be held at 11 a.m. Sunday, July 19. Performers must be 18 or older.

Auditions for *The Rocky Horror Show Live!* will also be held at 11 a.m. Sunday, July 19. Performers must be 18 or older.

All auditions will take place at [[venue:Greenlight Theatre Company]], 8021 Philips Highway, Suite 10, in Jacksonville.

More information is available at [greenlighttheatreco.com/auditions](https://www.greenlighttheatreco.com/auditions).
