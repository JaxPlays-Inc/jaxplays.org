Join [[theatre:The Island Theater]] for auditions for *Little Women*. Step into the timeless story of the March Sisters and be a part of a beautiful theatrical experience.

Auditions will be held at 7 p.m. Sunday, Sept. 20, and Monday, Sept. 21.

Performers should prepare a one- to two-minute monologue in the style of the show, but not from the show. The monologue does not need to be memorized, but auditioners should be comfortable and familiar with the material.

Auditions will also include cold readings from the script. Auditioners should be prepared to stay and read so the production team can see how the characters interact.

### Video Auditions

Unable to attend in person? Video auditions will be accepted through Friday, Sept. 25.

More information, including character descriptions, is available at [theislandtheater.com](https://www.theislandtheater.com).
