[[theatre:Players by the Sea]] is seeking actors to complete the cast of the new romantic comedy *I Love You, You're Perfect....Now Sign Here!*.

Three roles remain to be cast. This is a volunteer, non-union community theater production. All roles are unpaid.

Interested performers should send a headshot, selfies accepted, and resume to [dsacks2010@gmail.com](mailto:dsacks2010@gmail.com).

Players by the Sea welcomes submissions from performers of all experience levels.

### Available Roles

**Hannah**  
Stage age: mid- to late 20s. Educated, witty and strong-willed, with a sharp sense of humor. Kind and forgiving to everyone except her mother.

**Michael**  
Stage age: mid- to late 20s. A genuinely good guy. Crazy in love with Hannah. Smart and clever, but happiest just being a normal guy.

**Rick**  
Stage age: mid- to late 20s. The anti-Michael, and somehow still Michael's best friend. Likes to think people see him more as a chauvinist than a feminist, but that's a facade. He loves women, even strong women.
