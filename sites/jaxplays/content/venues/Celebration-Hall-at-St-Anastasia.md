Celebration Hall at St. Anastasia is an event venue on the St. Anastasia Catholic Church campus in St. Augustine Beach. The hall can host gatherings of up to 300 people and offers a performance stage, state-of-the-art sound and media capabilities, high-speed fiber-optic Wi-Fi, meeting rooms, a catering kitchen and flexible seating for presentations, dining and public events.

Located on Anastasia Island near scenic ponds and the St. Anastasia campus, the venue serves weddings, receptions, conferences, concerts, nonprofit events and community gatherings.
