Adam Fields came to theatre the long way around. After moving to Jacksonville in 2018 fresh out of high school, he spent years juggling odd jobs and playing college football before discovering a passion for acting. With no formal training, he threw himself into the local community theatre circuit — building his craft at Theatre Jacksonville, Orange Park Community Theatre, and Players by the Sea.

When the Alhambra Theatre & Dining began casting more local performers during the COVID era, Fields seized the opportunity. His first professional production there was *It's a Wonderful Life*, launching a string of Alhambra credits that gave him a crash course in professional rehearsal and performance. In May 2026, he returned to Jacksonville with the national tour of *Beetlejuice: the Musical* as part of the FSCJ Artist Series at the Moran Theater — the very stage he once watched from the audience, dreaming of being on it.
