Ally Ingraham is a Jacksonville-based performer with experience spanning musical theatre, jazz and choral performance. Most recently, she appeared as Tina and a Featured Ensemble Nun in [[production:2025 Sister Act]] at [[theatre:Players by the Sea]], showcasing her powerful vocals, dynamic choreography and expressive stage presence. A graduate of Douglas Anderson School of the Arts and former member of Florida State University’s Acaphiliacs A Cappella Group and Concert Choir, Ally has performed in venues ranging from the Times-Union Center to Walt Disney World’s Candlelight Processional, sharing the stage with renowned narrators such as Whoopi Goldberg and Neil Patrick Harris. With a vocal range spanning mezzo-soprano to alto, Ally brings passion, energy and precision to every performance across gospel, pop, Broadway and classical styles.
