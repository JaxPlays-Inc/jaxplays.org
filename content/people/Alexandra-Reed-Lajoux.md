Alexandra Reed Lajoux, mezzo-soprano, had the role of a lifetime when she played Mother Abbess in *The Sound of Music* at [[theatre: Amelia Musical Playhouse]] in August 2021, directed by [[person:Jill Dillingham]]. Her most recent roles there were Snug the Joiner/Lion in *Midsummer Night's Dream* (July 2025, directed by Joey Johnson), and Haley Harty in *Scrooge the Musical*, directed by Jimmy Galaviz.
