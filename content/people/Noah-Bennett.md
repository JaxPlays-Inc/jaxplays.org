Noah Bennett is a theatre artist, playwright, and performer based in Jacksonville, Florida. He holds an MFA from The Ohio State University and a BA from the University of Florida, and works across disciplines as an actor, writer, director, and educator. A board member of Lumen Repertory Theatre, his work is rooted in the belief that theatre is meant to be experienced, not consumed.
