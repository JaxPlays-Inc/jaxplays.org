Tyler Hammond is an actor, designer and director in North Florida. He is the technical director at Limelight Theatre and a card-holding member of The International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees, Local 115. Recent production credits in North Florida include [[production:2026 The Battle Of Shallowford|The Battle of Shallowford]] as technical director, [[production:2026 Once On This Island|Once On This Island]] as technical director, [[production:2026 Godspell|Godspell]] as technical director, [[production:2026 God of Carnage|God of Carnage]] as technical director, [[production:2026 The Fever|The Fever]] as technical director, [[production:2025 Our Town|Our Town]] as lighting designer, [[production:2025 A Moon for the Misbegotten|A Moon for the Misbegotten]] as director, lighting designer and scenic designer, [[production:2025 American Buffalo|American Buffalo]] as director and technical director, *Shakespeare Sampler* as technical director, [[production:2025 Good People|Good People]] as scenic designer, [[production:2024 Bakersfield Mist|Bakersfield Mist]] as scenic designer, [[production:2024 9 to 5: The Musical|9 to 5: The Musical]] as lighting designer, [[production:2024 Wait Until Dark|Wait Until Dark]] as director, technical director, lighting designer and sound designer, and [[production:2023 No Exit|No Exit]] as master carpenter.
