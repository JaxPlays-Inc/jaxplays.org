Carol has been performing in musical and theater productions since the age of 7. She recently returned to the stage as Marmee March in Troupe 1776’s productions of *Little Women* and *Good Wives*. Favorite roles include Ouiser and Clairee in *Steel Magnolias*, and Val in *A Chorus Line* (she won’t mention the year!) Carol is a Nurse Anesthetist in Jacksonville and considers her work in the arts her favorite hobby! She has 2 grown children and 2 beautiful granddaughters who she can’t wait to get on the stage!
