Hillary Hickam is a stage and screen actor whose career began in Dallas, Texas and has taken her from New York to Los Angeles. At the Alhambra Theatre & Dining, her credits include *Brighton Beach Memoirs*, *The Glass Menagerie*, and *Arsenic and Old Lace*.
