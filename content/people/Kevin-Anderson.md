Kevin Anderson is an acclaimed stage and screen actor whose work spans Broadway, Off-Broadway, London’s West End, Dublin, film and television. His notable credits include *Orphans* with Albert Finney, *Sleeping with the Enemy* with Julia Roberts, *Sunset Boulevard* with Patti LuPone, *Hoffa* opposite Jack Nicholson, and *Death of a Salesman* as Biff. Television audiences may also recognize him as Father Ray in ABC’s *Nothing Sacred*.

Over the course of his career, Anderson has received Tony, Golden Globe and Outer Critics Circle nominations, and has won honors including the Outer Critics Circle Award, Drama Desk Award, TV Critics Award, Joseph Jefferson Award, Theatre World Award and Viewers for Quality Television Award. He has also been a Steppenwolf Ensemble member for more than four decades.
