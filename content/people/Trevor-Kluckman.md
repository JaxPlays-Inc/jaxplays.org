Born in Aberdeen, Scotland, Trevor Kluckman holds a BA in Theatre Arts from Marymount Manhattan College. New York City credits include Tennessee Williams' *The Pink Bedroom* at Theatre Row, directed by Matthew Lillard; Mark Antony in *Julius Caesar* at Hudson Warehouse; and *Where's My Money?* at Cherry Lane Theatre. Regional credits include [[production:2026 The Pillowman|The Pillowman]] at Lumen Repertory Theatre, [[production:2025 Rhinoceros|Rhinoceros]] at Limelight Theatre, *Venus in Fur* at Wolfbane Productions and *Mothers and Sons* at A Classic Theatre. Trevor has appeared on ABC's *One Life to Live* and in the indie romcom *April Flowers*, streamable on Apple TV.
