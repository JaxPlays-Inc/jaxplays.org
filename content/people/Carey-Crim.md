Carey Crim is an award-winning playwright and screenwriter whose work has been produced across the United States, Europe, and Canada. Her plays have been staged at theatres including Purple Rose Theatre, Bay Street Theater, Rubicon Theatre, Shakespeare & Company, Florida Repertory Theatre, Gulfshore Playhouse, Riverside Theatre, and Miners Alley.

Her play *Wake* was adapted into a feature film, and her writing work also includes screenplays and television pilot scripts. Crim is represented by Mark Orsini at Bret Adams and Brandy Rivers at Amplified.
