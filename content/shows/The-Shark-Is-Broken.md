*The Shark is Broken* co-written by Ian Shaw, delves into the behind-the-scenes challenges during the making of the groundbreaking film 'Jaws.' Set on a boat off the coast of Martha's Vineyard, the play reveals the tense interactions among actors Robert Shaw, Roy Scheider and Richard Dreyfuss as they grapple with a notoriously difficult shoot, characterized by mechanical failures and the pressures of an escalating budget. The script cleverly blends humor and drama, mirroring the on-screen tension with the actors' off-screen conflicts and camaraderie.
