DramaWorks is the theater production arm of Florida State College at Jacksonville, known for showcasing a wide array of plays and musicals that both entertain and provoke thought. With a focus on student and community involvement, it serves as a creative hub for aspiring actors, directors, and technicians in the Jacksonville area.
