ShepardFest was founded in 2019 by theatre artists Raines Carr and Andrea McCook as a celebration of the life and work of Sam Shepard and the artists drawn to his uncompromising voice.

The festival brings artists, scholars, students and audiences together through performances, readings, films, music and conversation. ShepardFest is rooted in the belief that Shepard's work is best experienced through a blend of performance, scholarship, dialogue and community, with St. Augustine serving as its natural home.
