[[theatre:Lumen Repertory Theatre]] is accepting auditions for its 2026 Apprentice Company as part of the world premiere production of *Antigone: Unmasked*, directed by [[person:Brian Niece]].

The paid professional opportunity allows rising high school juniors and seniors, ages 16-21, and college students to train and perform alongside a professional ensemble while contributing to a new work in active development.

Auditions will be held June 1 and 4, 2026, from 7-8 p.m. at [[venue:San Marco Church]]. No monologue is required.

Four apprentices will be selected as part of Lumen Rep's NEXTGEN initiative, which invests in the next generation of theatre artists through professional training, collaboration and performance experience.

Audition signup is available through [SignUpGenius](https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10C0948AEAE2CA2F4C61-64061068-auditions).
