[[theatre:Greenlight Theatre Company]] will hold vocal auditions for *The Hunchback of Notre Dame* at 7 p.m. Tuesday, June 9.

Auditions will take place at [[venue:Greenlight Theatre Company]], 8021 Philips Highway, Suite 10, in Jacksonville.

The company describes the musical as one of the most powerful and breathtaking musicals ever written, with stunning music, epic storytelling and roles for a wide range of performers.

Audition information and sign-ups are available at [greenlighttheatreco.com/auditions](https://www.greenlighttheatreco.com/auditions).

Greenlight Theatre Company's mission is to engage, educate and inspire the community through high-quality, inclusive productions, classes and other programming for all ages and abilities.
