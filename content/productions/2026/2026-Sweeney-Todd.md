Greenlight Theatre, in collaboration with Swoop Troupe, presents *Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street*, Stephen Sondheim's darkly comic musical thriller.

The production follows a wronged barber who returns to London seeking revenge, only to find his fury entangled with Mrs. Lovett's pie shop and a city already hungry for blood. Swoop frames the show as a descent into the underbelly of London, where sardonic humor, sweeping romance and feverish despair collide.
