*She Loves Me* follows feuding parfumerie clerks Amalia and Georg, who unknowingly fall for each other through anonymous letters.

Set in a 1930s Budapest shop, the musical pairs sharp workplace comedy with a tender romance built on misunderstanding, vulnerability, and longing.
