Greenlight Theatre Company presents *Grease* at its Phillips Highway Jax Onstage location.

The musical follows Danny, Sandy, the Pink Ladies, and the Burger Palace Boys through the last days of high school, where summer romance meets teenage swagger, friendship, and classic 1950s rock-and-roll.

The source lists split casting for several roles: Lauren Manacup and Carolina Bello appear May 22, 24, and 30; Sophia Dorsey and Gabby Valerio appear May 23, 29, and 31; Mickey Titus appears May 22-24; Caitlin Allen appears as Patty May 22-24 and 31; Nayya Murray appears as Patty May 29 and 30.
