When U.S. Army Captain Jack Ludwig begins corresponding with Louise Rabiner, an aspiring actor and dancer in New York City, a hopeful connection grows across distance and uncertainty.

Inspired by the courtship of playwright Ken Ludwig's parents, *Dear Jack, Dear Louise* traces a relationship built through letters from June 1942 to May 1945 as war threatens to keep the pair apart.
