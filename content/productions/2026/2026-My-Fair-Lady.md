*My Fair Lady* follows Eliza Doolittle, a Cockney flower seller whose life changes when phonetics professor Henry Higgins bets he can pass her off as a lady.

The Lerner and Loewe musical pairs wit, romance, and questions of class as Eliza pushes beyond Higgins' experiment to claim her own voice.
