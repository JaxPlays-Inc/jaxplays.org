Saltwater Performing Arts stages Oscar Wilde's *The Importance of Being Earnest*, the enduring comedy where two charming bachelors create alternate identities and promptly lose control of the lie.

With rapid-fire wordplay, mistaken names, romantic reversals and the formidable Lady Bracknell presiding over the chaos, Wilde's play remains one of the sharpest and funniest portraits of social rules gone deliciously sideways.
