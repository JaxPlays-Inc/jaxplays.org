*Every Brilliant Thing* invites audiences into a story about love, loss, and the ordinary joys that make life bearable. After a mother's attempted suicide, a child begins writing a list of everything brilliant in the world; as the child grows, the list becomes a way to hold onto hope.

This one-person production is performed in rotation by Lindsay Curry and Jillianne Tamillo. PBTS lists the performer for each date on the production page.

**Content advisory:** PBTS notes sensitive subject matter including suicide and depression. Recommended ages 14 and up.
