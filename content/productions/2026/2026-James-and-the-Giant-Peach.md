Flagler College Department of Theater Arts brings *James and the Giant Peach* to Lewis Auditorium in a musical adaptation with music and lyrics by Benj Pasek and Justin Paul and book by Timothy Allen McDonald.

After a magic potion transforms an ordinary peach into something enormous, James finds himself traveling with a crew of human-sized insects. Their voyage is full of danger, imagination, and unlikely friendship as the group learns to work together on the way to a new home.
