City Repertory Theatre presents *Line & All in the Timing*, a double bill of absurdity and wit. Israel Horovitz's *Line*, his longest-running Off-Broadway play, turns a mysterious queue into a comic test of human ambition as everyone fights to be first.

The evening pairs Horovitz's biting setup with David Ives' *All in the Timing*, a fast-moving collection of one-act comedies built on clever language, quirky scenarios, and surreal theatrical turns.
