Saltwater Performing Arts brings Rodgers and Hammerstein's *Cinderella* to its Mainstage program with a version that keeps the enchantment and gives the familiar heroine a sharper, more self-possessed voice.

The musical follows Ella as a night at the ball opens the door to romance, courage and civic change. With lush melodies, comic court intrigue and a heroine who challenges the prince to see his kingdom clearly, this *Cinderella* finds magic in both transformation and conviction.
