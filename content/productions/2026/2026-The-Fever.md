Written by Wallace Shawn, *The Fever* places the audience inside a spiraling moral confession. Lumen Rep's intimate staging at BABS' LAB turns the room itself into part of the confrontation, challenging the distance between observer and participant as the speaker wrestles with privilege, global suffering, and the cost of looking away.
