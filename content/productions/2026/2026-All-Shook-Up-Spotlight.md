Spotlight Events Center is listed by Theatrical Rights Worldwide for a November 2026 production of *All Shook Up*.

Inspired by the songs of Elvis Presley, the musical drops a charismatic roustabout into a rule-bound town and lets love, rebellion and rock-and-roll do the rest. The result is a jukebox comedy full of mistaken identity, restless hearts and familiar songs with a theatrical twist.
