Spotlight Events Center is listed by Music Theatre International for a May 2026 production of *How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying*, Frank Loesser's Pulitzer Prize-winning musical satire of corporate ambition.

The show follows J. Pierrepont Finch as he uses a business advice book to rise from window washer to executive, turning office hierarchy, romance and company politics into bright, brassy comedy.
