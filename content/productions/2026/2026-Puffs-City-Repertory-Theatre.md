*Puffs* follows a group of well-meaning underdog students at a certain school of magic as they navigate friendship, classes and the chaos unfolding around them.

City Repertory Theatre describes the show as a fast-paced parody with physical comedy, quick character changes and ensemble energy. Performances are scheduled July 17-26, 2026.
