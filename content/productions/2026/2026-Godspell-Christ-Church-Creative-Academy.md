Christ Church Creative Academy presents *Godspell* at Christ Church Ponte Vedra Beach.

The musical, created by John-Michael Tebelak with music and lyrics by Stephen Schwartz, retells parables from the Gospel of Matthew through songs, sketches and a communal theatrical spirit.

The AllEvents listing schedules the production for Saturday, February 28, 2026 at 7:00 p.m. at 400 San Juan Drive in Ponte Vedra Beach. Christ Church's Spring 2026 Lookbook notes that the Creative Academy presents musical productions each year with casts drawn from parishioners and the community.
