*Come From Away* is based on the true story of Gander, Newfoundland, where 38 planes were diverted on September 11, 2001.

As thousands of stranded passengers arrive, a small community responds with food, shelter, music, and open-hearted welcome, revealing the possibility of connection in a moment of global crisis.
