Sibling rivalry, adultery and dungeons collide in *The Lion in Winter*, James Goldman's modern classic about the Plantagenet family.

Set during Christmas 1183, the play finds King Henry II, Eleanor of Aquitaine and their sons locked in a free-for-all over the future of a kingdom. Theatre Jacksonville presents the production as The Classic in San Marco for Season 107.
