Saltwater Performing Arts' Junior program presents *Madagascar: A Musical Adventure JR.*, the stage musical inspired by DreamWorks Animation's zoo-break comedy.

When four pampered Central Park Zoo residents unexpectedly find themselves on the journey of a lifetime, the result is a bright, comic adventure about friendship, change and learning to move it in a whole new world.
