*Much Ado About Nothing* follows two tangled courtships as sharp-tongued Beatrice and Benedick spar their way toward love while Hero and Claudio are pushed toward disaster by a malicious lie.

All the Devils Shakespeare stages this 2026 revival across three venues, opening with two May 17 performances at Larimer Arts Center in Palatka, moving to The Waterworks in St. Augustine on May 22 and 23, and closing with a free May 24 matinee at Howard Gilman Waterfront Park in St. Marys.
