The 5 & Dime Songbook is a one-night cabaret fundraiser celebrating the company's first 15 years and supporting its next chapter.

Hosted by Josh Waller, directed by Cole Marshall and music directed by Erin Barnes, the evening features songs, stories, adult beverages, hors d'oeuvres and 15th-anniversary swag.
