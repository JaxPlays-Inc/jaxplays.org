Saltwater Performing Arts announces *Finding Neverland* as its fall Mainstage production, inviting performers and audiences into the story of J. M. Barrie and the family who helped inspire *Peter Pan*.

The musical follows the meeting of reality and imagination as grief, play and creative courage begin to transform ordinary life into Neverland. Saltwater notes that the production will also have an opportunity to perform a 60-minute version at the Georgia Theatre Conference in October.
