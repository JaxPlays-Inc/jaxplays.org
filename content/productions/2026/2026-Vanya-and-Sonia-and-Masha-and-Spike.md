Vanya and Sonia have spent years in their childhood home, living quiet, resentful lives shadowed by missed chances and family obligations. Their movie-star sister Masha arrives with her young boyfriend Spike, stirring up old rivalries, unfulfilled dreams and a possible plan to sell the family estate.

Christopher Durang's Chekhov-inflected comedy turns sibling tension, longing and regret into a madcap family collision.
