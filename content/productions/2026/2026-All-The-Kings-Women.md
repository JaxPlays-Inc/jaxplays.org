Luigi Jannuzzi's *All The King's Women* looks at Elvis Presley's cultural shadow through a series of funny, heartfelt vignettes and monologues.

Across significant Elvis years, the play follows women whose encounters with fame, generosity, and simple kindness ripple into life-changing moments.
