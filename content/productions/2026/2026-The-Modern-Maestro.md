Saltwater Performing Arts opens its Spring 2026 Season 12 lineup with *The Modern Maestro*, a live tribute to Lin-Manuel Miranda's contemporary musical theatre world. The revue brings together Latin beats, Broadway drive and the creative spark behind work such as *Hamilton*, *In the Heights* and *Encanto*.

Built as a family-friendly celebration of a modern Broadway voice, the production showcases Saltwater performers in a fast-moving evening of music, storytelling and theatrical energy.
