Spotlight Events Center is listed by Theatrical Rights Worldwide for an August 2026 production of *Big Fish: Small Cast Edition*.

The musical follows Edward Bloom, a traveling salesman whose life seems to be made of giants, witches, mermaids and impossible adventures. As his son Will prepares to become a father, he tries to separate fact from myth and discovers that love can live inside a story even when the details refuse to behave.
