Flagler College Department of Theater Arts stages *Twelve Angry Jurors*, Sherman L. Sergel's adaptation of Reginald Rose's courtroom classic, at Lewis Auditorium.

A 19-year-old defendant appears to face an open-and-shut case until one juror begins questioning the testimony. Inside the jury room, certainty gives way to conflict as the group revisits the evidence, exposes personal bias, and confronts the responsibility of deciding another person's fate.
