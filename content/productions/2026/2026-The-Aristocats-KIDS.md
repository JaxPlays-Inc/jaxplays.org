Saltwater Performing Arts' KIDS program presents *Disney's The Aristocats KIDS*, a jazzy, feel-good musical about family, friendship and finding the way home.

When Duchess and her kittens are stranded far from Paris, they join forces with the smooth-talking Thomas O'Malley and a band of streetwise cats. The result is a bright, playful adventure designed for young performers and audiences ready for a little feline swing.
