Saltwater Performing Arts' Encore program presents *Come From Away*, the ensemble musical inspired by the true story of thousands of travelers grounded in Gander, Newfoundland after September 11, 2001.

As fear and uncertainty ripple around the world, a small community opens its homes, schools and gathering places to strangers. The musical moves quickly between dozens of lives, finding humor, grief and generosity in an extraordinary act of hospitality.
