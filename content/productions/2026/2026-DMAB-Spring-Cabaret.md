Don't Miss A Beat's 2026 Spring Cabaret brings the student company to the Ritz Theatre for a Broadway-themed concert.

The organization describes the production as a joyful journey through the heart of Broadway, featuring songs from beloved classics and modern hits while celebrating the storytelling, emotion and spectacle of American musical theatre.
