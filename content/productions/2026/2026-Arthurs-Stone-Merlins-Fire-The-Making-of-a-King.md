*Arthur's Stone, Merlin's Fire: The Making of a King* thrusts a young Arthur into a world of ancient magic, rising danger, and the weight of prophecy.

As Merlin guides him toward a destiny he never wanted, loyalties are tested and the fate of a kingdom hangs in the balance.
