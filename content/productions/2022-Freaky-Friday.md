"Freaky Friday" is a musical based on the classic story by Mary Rodgers. With music by Tom Kitt, lyrics by Brian Yorkey, and book by Bridget Carpenter, it tells the story of a mother and daughter who magically switch bodies and have to learn to see the world from each other's perspective.
