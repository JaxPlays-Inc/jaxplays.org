In *The 39 Steps*, Richard Hannay's ordinary life is upended when a mysterious woman tells him she is a spy and is soon murdered in his home.

With a shadowy organization on his trail, Hannay is pulled into a nationwide chase in Patrick Barlow's comic adaptation, built for quick changes, inventive stagecraft and high-speed theatrical mayhem.
