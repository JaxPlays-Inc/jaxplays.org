Young Oliver Adams is on the brink of literary success, and Broadway playwrights Charlotte and Arthur Sanders have already adapted his novel *The Angel Next Door* for the stage.

When they gather for a weekend in Newport with leading lady Margot Bell, the thin walls of a mansion reveal secrets, romance and theatrical chaos in Paul Slade Smith's comedy.
