Becky Foster is caught in middle age, middle management and a middling marriage, with no clear prospect of change. Then a socially inept and grief-struck millionaire wanders into the car dealership where she works, offering Becky the possibility of a new life.

Steven Dietz's comedy invites the audience to ride shotgun through a devious, delightful romp down the road not taken.

Production Note: This production is not suitable for young audience.
